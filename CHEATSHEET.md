## 🔄 Dialectical Method · 辩证方法

```
观察 OBSERVE  → 调查 Investigate the concrete situation
  ↓
分析 ANALYZE   → 分析矛盾 Identify all contradictions
  ↓
抓主要 PRIORITIZE → 抓主要矛盾 Find the principal one
  ↓
解决 RESOLVE   → 具体解决 Resolve with specific method
  ↓
检验 VERIFY    → 实践检验 Test in practice → Loop
```

---

## 📐 Contradiction Analysis · 矛盾分析 Template

```
1. List all contradictions 列出所有矛盾
2. Which is principal? 哪个是主要的？
3. Which aspect dominates? 哪方面占主导？
4. What would flip it? 什么条件下会转化？
5. What specific method? 具体解决办法？
```

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|

| 纸老虎 Paper tiger | Something that appears powerful but is hollow; strategically insignificant |
| 本本主义 Book worship | Treating texts as absolute truth without testing against reality |
| 教条主义 Dogmatism | Mechanical application of theory without considering concrete conditions |
| 经验主义 Empiricism | Relying purely on experience without extracting principles |
| 游击战 Guerrilla warfare | Mobile, decentralized warfare by small units against a larger force |
| 持久战 Protracted war | Extended conflict passing through stages: defense → stalemate → counter-offensive |
| 整风 Rectification | Organizational self-correction through study, criticism, and reform |
