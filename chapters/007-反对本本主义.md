# 反对本本主义
*Oppose Book Worship*

**Date:** 一九三 ○年五月

---

## Key Argument / 核心论点

Anti-dogmatism manifesto: "No investigation, no right to speak." Investigation is the mother of solution — all conclusions come at the end of investigation, not at the beginning. Opposes book worship (本本主义/教条主义) and blind empiricism. Establishes investigation-and-research as the foundational method of correct leadership.
