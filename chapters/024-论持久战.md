# 论持久战
*On Protracted War*

**Date:** 一九三八年五月


## Key Argument / 核心论点

Masterwork of strategic analysis. Systematically refutes both "national subjugation theory" (亡国论) and "quick victory theory" (速胜论). Proposes three-stage protracted war: strategic defensive → strategic stalemate → strategic counter-offensive. Core insight: time favors the side that can mobilize the masses and wage people's war. Emphasizes subjective initiative (能动性) within objective constraints.
