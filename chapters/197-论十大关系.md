# 论十大关系
*On the Ten Major Relationships*

**Date:** 一九五六年四月二十五日


## Key Argument / 核心论点

Systems-thinking applied to socialist construction. Drawing lessons from Soviet experience, Mao analyzes ten key relationships (heavy vs. light industry, coastal vs. inland, central vs. local, etc.) with the fundamental principle of "mobilizing all positive factors." A masterclass in seeing interconnections and balancing competing priorities within a complex system.
