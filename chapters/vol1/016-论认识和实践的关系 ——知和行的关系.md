# 实践论 · 论认识和实践的关系
*On Practice: The Relationship of Knowledge and Action*

**Date:** July 1937 | 一九三七年七月
**Significance:** Epistemological foundation — paired with *On Contradiction* as the twin pillars of Mao's philosophy

---

## Key Argument

The dialectical relationship between knowledge and practice. Mao argues that knowledge originates in practice, ascends from perceptual to rational understanding, and must return to practice for verification. This forms an infinite, deepening spiral: **practice -> knowledge -> practice -> knowledge...**

The essay targets two errors: **dogmatism** (教条主义: theory without practice — treating books as absolute truth) and **empiricism** (经验主义: practice without theory — relying solely on raw experience without extracting principles).

**Core thesis:** "Practice, knowledge, again practice, again knowledge. This form repeats itself in endless cycles, and with each cycle the content of practice and knowledge rises to a higher level." (实践、认识、再实践、再认识，这种形式，循环往复以至无穷，而实践和认识之每一循环的内容，都比较地进到了高一级的程度。)

---

## Structure & Key Concepts

### I. Practice as Source / 实践是认识的来源

Human knowledge primarily depends on **material production activity** (生产活动). Before anything else, humans must eat, shelter, and survive. This productive activity is the most fundamental form of practice and the primary source of all knowledge.

**Other forms of practice:** class struggle, political life, scientific experimentation, artistic creation. Each reveals different aspects of reality.

**Key implication:** You cannot understand something purely through reading or speculation. "If you want to know the taste of a pear, you must change the pear by eating it yourself." (你要知道梨子的滋味，你就得变革梨子，亲口吃一吃。)

### II. Perceptual to Rational / 感性认识→理性认识

Knowledge develops through **two leaps:**

| Stage | Nature | Function | Limitation |
|-------|--------|----------|------------|
| **Perceptual** (感性) | Raw sense-data: sights, sounds, experiences, surface phenomena | Provides the raw material of knowledge | Cannot grasp essence, only surface |
| **Rational** (理性) | Concepts, judgments, inferences — the extraction of patterns, laws, and essence | Reveals the internal connections and underlying logic | Can become empty abstraction if disconnected from perceptual base |

**Both are necessary; neither alone is sufficient.** Perceptual without rational = blind. Rational without perceptual = empty.

### III. The Two Leaps / 两次飞跃

**First leap:** Perceptual knowledge -> rational knowledge. This is the **qualitative transformation** from sensing to understanding. It requires: (1) sufficient perceptual material; (2) mental processing (去粗取精、去伪存真、由此及彼、由表及里).

**Second leap:** Rational knowledge -> practice. This is the **more important** leap, because:
1. Only practice can verify whether rational knowledge is correct
2. The purpose of knowledge is to guide action and transform reality

### IV. Practice as Criterion of Truth / 实践是检验真理的标准

"Only social practice can be the criterion of truth." (只有人们的社会实践，才是人们对于外界认识的真理性的标准。)

A theory that cannot be tested in practice is not useful knowledge. Success confirms correctness; failure reveals error and points toward correction.

### V. The Infinite Spiral / 无限螺旋

Knowledge is never complete. Each cycle of practice-knowledge-practice deepens understanding but reveals new unknowns. The process is **infinite, iterative, and upward-spiraling** — never a closed circle.

**Three stages of relative truth:**
1. Discovered through practice
2. Verified by practice
3. To be further developed and deepened through continued practice

## Methodological Imperatives

| Principle | Chinese | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Investigation above assumption | 调查>假设 | No investigation, no right to speak |
| Theory-practice integration | 理论联系实际 | Theory must be tested against and enriched by practice |
| Discard-dross-select-essential | 去粗取精 | Filter noise, extract signal |
| Eliminate-false-retain-true | 去伪存真 | Verify against reality, discard deception |
| Surface-to-interior | 由表及里 | Move from phenomena to essence |
| Concrete-to-abstract-to-concrete | 具体→抽象→具体 | Rich, concrete starting point -> abstract patterns -> return to concrete reality |

## Application Template / 应用指南

1. **INVESTIGATE** — gather firsthand data through direct engagement (调查)
2. **STUDY** — process raw data into patterns, concepts, principles (研究)
3. **DECIDE** — form concrete action plans based on rational analysis (决策)
4. **ACT** — execute (执行)
5. **REFLECT** — compare results with expectations (总结)
6. **LOOP** — return to step 1 with deeper understanding
