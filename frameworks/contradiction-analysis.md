# Contradiction Analysis · 矛盾论

> *"The law of contradiction in things, that is, the law of the unity of opposites, is the fundamental law of nature and of society."*
> *"事物矛盾的法则，即对立统一的法则，是自然和社会的根本法则。"*

## Core Concepts

### 1. The Universality of Contradiction · 矛盾的普遍性

Contradiction exists in all things and throughout their development. **Every situation contains opposing forces.** The question is not *whether* contradiction exists, but *what form* it takes.

**Application:** When analyzing any situation, start by identifying the opposing forces at play. Don't look for harmony — look for tension.

### 2. The Particularity of Contradiction · 矛盾的特殊性

Each contradiction has its own specific characteristics. **No two contradictions are identical.** The method of resolution must match the specific nature of the contradiction.

**Application:** Don't apply the same solution to every problem. Study the specifics before acting.

### 3. The Principal Contradiction · 主要矛盾

In a complex situation with many contradictions, **one is always principal** — its existence and development determines or influences the others. Solving the principal contradiction unlocks everything else.

**Decision Heuristic:**
1. List all contradictions in the situation
2. Ask: "Which one, if resolved, would most affect the others?"
3. Focus resources there

### 4. The Principal Aspect · 矛盾的主要方面

Within each contradiction, one aspect is principal — it dominates. **The nature of a thing is determined by the principal aspect.** When the principal aspect changes, the nature of the thing changes.

**Application:** For any "A vs B" tension, ask: "Which side currently dominates? Under what conditions would that flip?"

### 5. Identity and Struggle · 同一性与斗争性

Opposites are both **united** (they presuppose each other) and in **struggle** (they push against each other). Under certain conditions, opposites transform into each other.

**Application:** "Bad" situations contain the seeds of "good" outcomes. Look for transformation conditions.

## The Dialectical Method · 辩证方法

```
1. OBSERVE    → 调查 (Investigate the situation)
2. ANALYZE     → 分析矛盾 (Identify all contradictions)
3. PRIORITIZE  → 抓主要矛盾 (Find the principal contradiction)
4. RESOLVE     → 解决矛盾 (Resolve it with specific methods)
5. VERIFY      → 实践检验 (Test in practice, return to step 1)
```

## Key Principles

| Principle | Chinese | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Internal cause is primary | 内因是根本 | External conditions work through internal contradictions |
| Qualitative change through quantitative accumulation | 量变到质变 | Persistent small actions lead to fundamental shifts |
| Negation of negation | 否定之否定 | Development is spiral, not linear — apparent setbacks are part of progress |
| Concrete analysis of concrete conditions | 具体问题具体分析 | No universal template; study each case |

## Red Flags (Anti-Patterns)

- **One-sidedness · 片面性**: Seeing only one aspect of a contradiction
- **Superficiality · 表面性**: Treating symptoms instead of the underlying contradiction
- **Subjectivism · 主观性**: Imposing preconceived frameworks instead of investigating reality
- **Mechanical application · 机械套用**: Applying formulas without studying specifics
