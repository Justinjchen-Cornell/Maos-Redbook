# Mass Line · 群众路线

> *"The masses are the real heroes, while we ourselves are often childish and ignorant."*
> *"群众是真正的英雄，而我们自己则往往是幼稚可笑的。"*

## The Method

```
FROM THE MASSES (从群众中来)
    ↓
Gather scattered, unsystematic opinions (收集分散的意见)
    ↓
CONCENTRATE & SYSTEMATIZE (集中整理)
    ↓
Form coherent policies, strategies (形成系统的主张)
    ↓
TO THE MASSES (到群众中去)
    ↓
Propagate, explain, test in action (宣传解释，化为行动)
    ↓
VERIFY & CORRECT (检验修正)
    ↓
Return to step 1
```

## Core Principles

### 1. Investigation Over Assumption · 调查 > 假设

**"没有调查，就没有发言权"** — No investigation, no right to speak. Before making any decision about people, go among them, observe, ask, listen.

**Application:** Never build products/strategies based on conference-room assumptions. Go to the field. Talk to real users/customers.

### 2. The Masses as Teachers · 群众是先生

**"只有做群众的学生，才能做群众的先生"** — Only by being the pupil of the masses can you be their teacher. Expertise without empathy is useless.

**Application:** Domain experts often miss what practitioners know instinctively. Humble yourself to learn from frontline people.

### 3. From Scattered to Systematic · 分散→系统

Raw feedback is disorganized. The leader's job is to **concentrate scattered ideas, systematize them, and return them** as actionable guidance.

**Application:** Your value-add is synthesis, not original genius. Listen to many → find the pattern → articulate it clearly → return it.

### 4. Patient Persuasion · 耐心说服

**"命令主义是错误的"** — Commandism is wrong. You cannot force understanding. Explain, demonstrate, let people arrive at conviction themselves.

**Application:** Top-down mandates fail without buy-in. Invest in persuasion, demonstration, and leading by example.

## Operational Method · 工作方法

| Step | Chinese | Action |
|------|---------|--------|
| Investigate | 调查 | Go to the field, gather firsthand data |
| Study | 研究 | Analyze, find patterns |
| Decide | 决定 | Form concrete, practical policies |
| Propagate | 宣传 | Explain clearly so everyone understands |
| Organize | 组织 | Mobilize action |
| Check | 检查 | Verify results, correct errors |
| Summarize | 总结 | Extract lessons, return to step 1 |

## Red Flags

- **Commandism · 命令主义**: Issuing orders without explanation or buy-in
- **Tailism · 尾巴主义**: Following the masses without leading — mistaking popular opinion for truth
- **Bureaucratism · 官僚主义**: Managing from a desk, disconnected from ground reality
- **Subjectivism · 主观主义**: Assuming you know what people need without asking
