# United Front · 统一战线

> *"Unite the many, isolate the few; unite all forces that can be united."*
> *"团结多数，孤立少数；团结一切可以团结的力量。"*

## The Framework

```
MAP THE FIELD (分清敌我友)
    ├── Core allies (可靠同盟) — build deep, trust-based relationships
    ├── Middle forces (中间势力) — win over through persuasion and shared interest  
    ├── Temporary allies (暂时同盟) — cooperate on specific issues only
    └── Hard opposition (顽固势力) — isolate, neutralize, defeat strategically
```

## Core Principles

### 1. Distinguish Friends from Enemies · 分清敌友

**"谁是我们的敌人？谁是我们的朋友？这个问题是革命的首要问题。"**
Who are our enemies? Who are our friends? This is the primary question.

**Application:** Before any coalition effort, map all stakeholders on a spectrum: Core → Friendly → Neutral → Skeptical → Hostile. Different strategies for each.

### 2. Independence Within Alliance · 独立自主

**"统一战线中的独立自主"** — Maintain independence within the united front. Alliance does not mean subordination. You retain your own organization, principles, and freedom of action.

**Application:** Partnerships are valuable, but never cede core autonomy. Have your own "red lines."

### 3. Unite and Struggle · 又联合又斗争

With allies who are not fully aligned: **unite on common ground, struggle on points of difference.** This is not contradiction — it's healthy tension management.

**Application:** With partners, agree on the shared goal but be explicit about boundaries. Constructive tension is better than fake harmony.

### 4. Develop Progressive Forces · 发展进步势力

**Build your own base first.** You can only be an effective coalition partner if you have independent strength. Weak partners get absorbed or discarded.

**Application:** Before seeking alliances, build your own capabilities. Strong partners respect strength.

### 5. Win Over the Middle · 争取中间势力

The "middle forces" are the decisive factor. They swing between sides. **The side that wins the middle wins the whole situation.**

**Application:** In any competitive landscape, identify the undecided/swing constituency. They are your highest-leverage target.

## Key Quotes

| Quote | Application |
|-------|-------------|
| "所谓政治，就是把朋友搞得多多的，敌人搞得少少的" | Politics is making more friends and fewer enemies |
| "以斗争求团结则团结存，以退让求团结则团结亡" | Struggle for unity preserves it; concession for unity destroys it |
| "利用矛盾，争取多数，反对少数，各个击破" | Exploit contradictions, win over the majority, oppose the minority, defeat them one by one |
