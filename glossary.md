# Glossary · 术语词典

> Key concepts from Mao Zedong's Selected Works, with bilingual definitions and usage context.

---

## Core Philosophical Terms · 核心哲学术语

### 实事求是 — Seek Truth From Facts

**Definition:** Base all decisions and theories on objective reality, verified through investigation and practice. Reject dogma, assumption, and wishful thinking.

**Context:** Formulated in 《改造我们的学习》(1941) as the antithesis of subjectivism. Became the epistemological foundation of Mao's method.

**Application:** Before any decision, ask: "What are the facts? Have I investigated? Can I verify?"

---

### 矛盾 — Contradiction

**Definition:** The unity and struggle of opposites inherent in all things. The fundamental driving force of development and change.

**Key Dimensions:**
- **主要矛盾** Principal contradiction — the one that dominates a complex situation
- **矛盾的主要方面** Principal aspect — the dominant side within a contradiction
- **对抗性/非对抗性** Antagonistic vs. non-antagonistic contradictions

**Context:** Fully developed in 《矛盾论》(1937). Rooted in Marxist dialectics but significantly expanded.

---

### 实践 — Practice

**Definition:** Conscious, purposeful human activity that changes objective reality — the source, test, and purpose of all knowledge.

**Context:** Central to 《实践论》(1937). Knowledge → Practice → Knowledge is the infinite spiral of understanding.

---

### 纸老虎 — Paper Tiger

**Definition:** Something that appears powerful and terrifying but is actually hollow, weak, and ultimately defeatable. Strategic confidence despite tactical caution.

**Context:** "一切反动派都是纸老虎" — All reactionaries are paper tigers (1946, interview with Anna Louise Strong). Later expanded to nuclear weapons and imperialism.

**Application:** Distinguish between apparent strength and real strength. Never be intimidated by surface power.

---

## Strategic Terms · 战略术语

### 游击战 — Guerrilla Warfare

**Definition:** Mobile, decentralized warfare where small units use surprise, terrain, and popular support against a conventionally superior force.

**16-Character Formula:**
> 敌进我退，敌驻我扰，敌疲我打，敌退我追

---

### 持久战 — Protracted War

**Definition:** An extended conflict where the weaker side passes through three stages: Strategic Defense → Strategic Stalemate → Strategic Counter-Offensive.

**Key Insight:** Time is on the side of the weaker party if they can survive and grow.

---

### 集中优势兵力 — Concentrate Superior Forces

**Definition:** Achieve absolute local superiority (ideally 6:1, 5:1, or at minimum 3:1) against a single enemy point, even if overall forces are inferior.

---

### 歼灭战 — Annihilation Warfare

**Definition:** The goal is to destroy enemy units completely, not merely drive them off. "伤其十指不如断其一指" — better to cut off one finger than injure ten.

---

## Political & Organizational Terms · 政治组织术语

### 群众路线 — Mass Line

**Definition:** The leadership method of gathering ideas from the masses, concentrating and systematizing them, then returning them to the masses for implementation and verification.

**Cycle:** 从群众中来 (From masses) → 集中起来 (Concentrate) → 到群众中去 (To masses) → 检验 (Verify)

---

### 统一战线 — United Front

**Definition:** A strategic alliance of diverse forces united around a common objective, where each party maintains independence and the leading force navigates between "uniting" and "struggling."

**Key Principle:** 又联合又斗争 — Unite and struggle simultaneously.

---

### 整风 — Rectification

**Definition:** Systematic organizational self-correction through study of theory, criticism and self-criticism, and reform of work style.

**Formula:** 团结—批评—团结 (Unity—Criticism—Unity)

---

### 民主集中制 — Democratic Centralism

**Definition:** Organizational principle combining broad democratic discussion with centralized decision-making and unified action. Democracy in discussion, centralism in execution.

---

## Methodological Terms · 方法论术语

### 本本主义 — Book Worship / Dogmatism

**Definition:** Treating written texts as absolute authority without testing against concrete reality. The opposite of 实事求是.

**Antidote:** 没有调查，就没有发言权 — No investigation, no right to speak.

---

### 教条主义 — Doctrinairism

**Definition:** Mechanical application of theoretical formulas without studying the specific characteristics of the concrete situation.

---

### 经验主义 — Empiricism (Pejorative)

**Definition:** Relying purely on personal experience without extracting principles or theoretical understanding. Blind practice without theory.

---

### 主观主义 — Subjectivism

**Definition:** The error of basing decisions on subjective desire rather than objective reality. The root error from which dogmatism and empiricism both stem.

---

### 调查研究 — Investigation and Research

**Definition:** The systematic process of gathering facts firsthand, analyzing them, and drawing conclusions. The foundation of all correct decision-making.

---

### 自力更生 — Self-Reliance

**Definition:** Relying on one's own strength as the primary basis, while not rejecting external assistance. Independence is not isolation.

---

## Decision Heuristics · 决策启发

| Concept | Quick Test |
|---------|------------|
| 实事求是 | "Do I have facts or just opinions?" |
| 主要矛盾 | "Which problem, if solved, unlocks the rest?" |
| 纸老虎 | "Is this strength real or just intimidating?" |
| 群众路线 | "Have I asked the people affected?" |
| 自力更生 | "Am I building independent capability?" |
